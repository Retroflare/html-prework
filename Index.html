Background

I was born and raised in Jamaica, a vibrant and culturally rich island in the Caribbean. From a young age, I developed a deep passion for architecture, which led me to pursue a degree in Architectural Studies during my time at university. The experience of studying architecture allowed me to explore my creativity and develop a keen eye for design. embarked on an exciting journey to the United States, seeking new opportunities and challenges. However, my passion for sports remained a significant part of my life. During my college years back home in Jamaica, I excelled as both a track star and a soccer player. I dedicated countless hours to training and honing my skills, dreaming of a professional career in sports. Unfortunately, injuries intervened, hindering my path to becoming a professional athlete. Although it was a setback, these challenges only fueled my determination to explore other avenues and channel my passion in different ways. It was during this time that I discovered my unwavering dedication to architecture and design. With a blend of my architectural knowledge, creative mindset, and the resilience forged through my sporting experiences, I am now equipped to tackle the diverse and dynamic challenges of the design world

Why I want to become a web developer

Web development has always fascinated me with its limitless possibilities and the ability to create something that can be accessed and used by people all around the world. I love the process of turning ideas into reality and creating engaging, user-friendly websites that provide value to others. I aspire to be a web developer because of my genuine passion for crafting digital experiences that leave a lasting impact. Web development presents a unique blend of creativity and technical proficiency that allows me to bring my ideas to life in the digital realm. The ability to design and build interactive websites fascinates me, as it provides a platform to connect people, facilitate communication, and solve real-world problems. As a web developer, I am driven by the constant evolution of technology, the endless opportunities for learning and growth, and the ability to make a tangible difference in people's lives through the websites I create. I am excited to collaborate with diverse teams, leverage cutting-edge technologies, and push the boundaries of what is possible on the web. Being a web developer means being part of a rapidly evolving industry that demands continuous innovation and adaptability, and I am thrilled to embark on this journey to contribute my skills, creativity, and dedication to shaping the digital landscape and making a meaningful impact on the online world.
